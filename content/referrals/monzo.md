## What is monzo?
Monzo is an online bank. But unlike all the old banks, it's built on modern technology. This allows them to do amazing things like give you instant notification of payments.

## My experience
I have used Monzo for a long time. So long that I was with them when they were called [Mondo](https://monzo.com/blog/2016/08/25/monzo/), and only offered a pre-paid card that needed topping up. Unlike today, where they are a [fully fledge bank](https://monzo.com/blog/2017/04/05/banking-licence/).

I recently switch my main account from Natwest to Monzo, and haven't had any regrets.

## My only issue
When I was on holiday, I got an notification from something I had ordered the week before, then another. I knew instantly something had gone wrong. I quickly opened the app and got talking to someone within minutes. They said that the payment would be sorted. Excellent, closed the chat and carryied on with my holiday.

Once I got back home, about two days later, I still hadn't been refunded the duplicate payment. I got talking to customer support again. Who quickly realised their original mistake and appolgised for it not being sorted sooner. Within about 30 minutes I had my money back.

A few weeks later I got a notification crediting £200 to my account. Not something I was expecting. While I tried to work out where it came from a chat notifcation popped up from customer services. They had looked into the issue and had decided to give me £200 to appologise.

Considering I never even complained or once got annoyed at the mistake, this was amazing. I only wish every customer service was this good.
